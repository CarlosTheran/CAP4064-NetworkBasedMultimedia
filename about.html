Common Exercises

The following are commons exercises that we encourage our clients to do as part of thier daily exercise rutine

Burpee
Burpee are a great, full body exercise to increase your strength and endurace. being in a standing position, deop into a squat and extend you hands forward, kick your feet back and then forward again quickly, and then jump up from a squatted position.
Plank
Plank build your core stregth. to perform a plank, get in a push up position and rest your forearms on the floor Hold the position as long as you can.
Mountain Climber
Mountain climbers are a good cardio exercise. place your hands on the floor in a push up position, then bring one knee up to you chest and then switch as quickly as you can.
